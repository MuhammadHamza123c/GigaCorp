SECTION: SHIPPING POLICY
================================================================================

GigaCorp offers standard (5–8 business days), expedited (2–3 business days), and overnight (next business day) shipping across all 50 US states. Standard shipping is free on orders over $50. Expedited shipping costs a flat rate of $12.99, and overnight shipping costs $24.99. International shipping is available to Canada, the UK, Australia, and Japan with rates calculated at checkout based on destination and package weight. All domestic orders placed before 2:00 PM EST on a business day are shipped the same day. Orders placed after 2:00 PM EST are shipped the following business day. GigaCorp does not ship to PO boxes for expedited or overnight orders. A signature may be required for orders over $500. Tracking information is emailed automatically once the carrier scans the package.

Standard shipping (5–8 business days) is free for orders of $50 or more. Orders under $50 incur a flat $5.99 shipping fee. Expedited shipping (2–3 business days) is a flat $12.99 regardless of order size. Overnight shipping (next business day by end of day) is $24.99 flat. Delivery dates are estimates and not guaranteed unless overnight shipping is selected. GigaCorp is not responsible for delays caused by weather, carrier disruptions, or incorrect addresses provided by the customer. If a package is returned to sender due to an incorrect address, the customer will be charged the return shipping cost plus a $5 restocking fee. Reshipment will require a new shipping charge.

For international shipments, the customer is responsible for all customs duties, taxes, and brokerage fees. GigaCorp declares the full value of the merchandise on the customs form and cannot mark items as gifts or lower the declared value. International delivery times vary by destination; typical ranges are 7–14 business days for Canada, 10–20 business days for the UK and Australia, and 5–12 business days for Japan. Expedited and overnight options are not available for international addresses. All international sales are final; returns are not accepted on international orders unless the item arrived damaged or defective. Claims for damaged or defective items must be submitted within 7 calendar days of delivery.

SECTION: RETURNS & REFUNDS POLICY
================================================================================

GigaCorp accepts returns on most items within 30 calendar days of the delivery date. To be eligible for a return, the item must be unused, in its original packaging, and accompanied by a receipt or proof of purchase. Items that are not in original condition, damaged, or missing parts for reasons not due to GigaCorp's error will be rejected. The customer is responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to a GigaCorp error (wrong item shipped or defective product). Return shipping labels are issued by GigaCorp customer support and cost a flat $6.99, deducted from the refund amount.

Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after the returned item is received and inspected by the warehouse. The refund is issued to the original payment method. Shipping charges are non-refundable except in cases where GigaCorp made an error. Original shipping fees are refunded only when the entire order is returned due to a GigaCorp mistake. Partial refunds may be granted for items returned without original packaging or with minor cosmetic damage, at GigaCorp's sole discretion.

Non-returnable items include: gift cards, downloadable software, personalized or custom-made products, opened personal care items, and items marked as final sale. Clearance items with a discount of 50% or more are final sale and cannot be returned. If you receive a defective item, please contact customer support within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the defect. GigaCorp will provide a prepaid return label and ship a replacement at no additional cost. If the replacement item is out of stock, a full refund will be issued including original shipping costs.

SECTION: BUSINESS HOURS
================================================================================

GigaCorp customer support is available Monday through Friday, 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM Eastern Time. Saturday hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM Eastern Time. Sunday support is available via email only; email responses on Sunday are handled within 4–6 hours. The customer support phone line (1-800-555-GIGA) is staffed during the hours listed above. Live chat through the GigaCorp website follows the same schedule as phone support. Email support (support@gigacorp.com) is monitored 24/7 and responses are sent within 2 hours during business hours and within 6 hours outside of business hours.

GigaCorp observes the following holidays and will be closed on these days: New Year's Day, Martin Luther King Jr. Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day (July 4), Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, the Friday after Thanksgiving, Christmas Eve (half day until 12:00 PM), Christmas Day, and New Year's Eve (half day until 12:00 PM). On half-day holidays, phone and chat support are available 7:00 AM to 12:00 PM Eastern Time. Email responses on full holidays may take up to 24 hours. GigaCorp's warehouse and shipping operations follow the same holiday schedule; no shipments are processed on observed holidays. Orders placed on a holiday are shipped the next business day.

Billing inquiries are handled by a separate department available Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM Eastern Time. For billing issues, email billing@gigacorp.com or call 1-800-555-BILL. Technical support for GigaCorp digital products is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week via the online help center at help.gigacorp.com. Phone technical support is available Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM Eastern Time. Premium and Enterprise tier customers receive dedicated priority phone lines with extended hours (see Service Tiers section).

SECTION: SERVICE TIERS
================================================================================

GigaCorp offers three customer service tiers: Standard, Premium, and Enterprise. Standard tier is included with every customer account at no additional charge and provides phone and chat support during regular business hours, email support with a 2-hour response window, and access to the online knowledge base. Standard tier customers are served on a first-come, first-served basis with an average phone wait time of 8–12 minutes during peak hours (11:00 AM – 3:00 PM EST weekdays).

Premium tier costs $9.99 per month or $99.99 per year (saving 16%). Premium benefits include priority phone queue with an average wait time of under 2 minutes, a dedicated customer support agent assigned to your account, extended phone hours (7:00 AM – 11:00 PM EST weekdays and 9:00 AM – 7:00 PM EST Saturdays), free return shipping on up to 10 returns per year (value up to $6.99 each), and exclusive access to the Premium customer support portal with live order tracking and one-click returns. Premium members also get early access to new product launches and sales events.

Enterprise tier is designed for business accounts with 10 or more active users. Pricing starts at $49.99 per month for up to 25 users and scales based on account size. Enterprise benefits include everything in Premium plus a dedicated account manager with quarterly business reviews, 24/7 priority phone and chat support (including holidays), API access for order management and inventory queries, custom onboarding and training sessions for your team, consolidated monthly billing with net-30 payment terms, and a 99.9% uptime SLA on support ticket response times. Enterprise accounts also receive volume pricing discounts on orders exceeding $10,000 annually. Contact enterprise@gigacorp.com to set up an Enterprise account or to upgrade from Premium.

SECTION: ACCOUNT & ORDER MANAGEMENT
================================================================================

Customers can create an account on the GigaCorp website using an email address and password. Accounts store shipping addresses, payment methods, and order history for faster checkout. GigaCorp uses industry-standard encryption (AES-256) to protect stored payment information. Credit card details are tokenized and never stored in plaintext. Customers can update their profile, change passwords, and manage saved addresses from the My Account dashboard. Two-factor authentication is available for enhanced account security and can be enabled under Account Settings.

To place an order, add items to the cart and proceed to checkout. GigaCorp accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, and Apple Pay. GigaCorp store credit and promotional gift cards can be applied at checkout. Orders are pending until payment is authorized and the fraud check is cleared. If a payment is declined, the customer receives an email with a link to update the payment method. Orders in pending status for more than 24 hours are automatically cancelled. Cancelled orders release any held inventory back to stock.

Order modifications (item quantity changes, address corrections) can be made within 1 hour of placement by contacting support. After 1 hour, the order enters processing and cannot be modified. To change an order after the 1-hour window, the customer must cancel and reorder. Cancellations are accepted for orders that have not yet shipped. Once an order has shipped, it must follow the standard return process. Customers can view order status and tracking information in the My Orders section of their account. Statuses include: Pending, Processing, Shipped, Out for Delivery, Delivered, and Cancelled.

SECTION: PRODUCT WARRANTY & REPAIRS
================================================================================

All GigaCorp branded hardware products come with a one-year limited warranty from the date of purchase. The warranty covers manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship under normal use. It does not cover damage caused by accident, misuse, unauthorized modifications, improper installation, power surges, normal wear and tear, or cosmetic damage that does not affect functionality. To file a warranty claim, contact customer support with the order number and a description of the issue. GigaCorp may require photos or video of the defect. If approved, GigaCorp will repair or replace the item at no charge. The customer pays shipping to GigaCorp; GigaCorp pays return shipping.

Extended warranty plans are available for purchase within 30 days of the original product purchase. The GigaCorp Care plan extends the warranty to two years and includes accidental damage coverage for drops and spills. GigaCorp Care Plus extends to three years with full accidental damage protection and includes a no-questions-asked replacement option (one per product lifetime). GigaCorp Care costs 10% of the product purchase price. GigaCorp Care Plus costs 18% of the product purchase price. Both plans include free shipping on all warranty and repair claims.

For out-of-warranty repairs, GigaCorp offers a flat-rate repair service. Customers can request a repair quote by submitting a support ticket. Flat-rate repair pricing starts at $39.99 for diagnostic evaluation (credited toward the repair cost if approved). Typical repair turnaround is 7–10 business days after the item is received at the service center. Repair services include a 90-day warranty on the repaired component. Expedited repair service (3–5 business days) is available for an additional $25 fee. GigaCorp does not offer repair services for products from other manufacturers. Genuine GigaCorp replacement parts can be ordered through the Parts Store at parts.gigacorp.com.
